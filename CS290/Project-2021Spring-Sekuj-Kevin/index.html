Jonatan Leandoer Håstad, also known as as Yung Lean, is a Swedish rapper, singer, and song writer.

Yung Lean was born in Minsk, Belarus, and raised in Stockholm, Sweden. He gained popularity in 2013 with his hit single "Ginseng Strip 2002", which went viral on YouTube. Leandoer's debut mixtape, Unknown Death 2002, was also released later that year, along with his debut studio album, Unknown Memory, in 2014.

Two years later, Yung Lean released his second mixtape, Frost God, and his second and most expansive album, Warlord, later that year. He released critically acclaimed album Stranger in 2017, featuring singles such as "Red Bottom Sky" and "Skimask." He followed up with his third mixtape, Poison Ivy, in 2018, with singles such as "Happy Feet." Yung Lean's most recent studio album to date is Starz, released in 2020.

Yung Lean is associated with the cloud rap, emo rap, and SoundCloud rap genres. He cites artists such as Lil B, 50 Cent, and Young Thug his major influences.
